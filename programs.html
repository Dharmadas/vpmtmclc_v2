Our college is approved by BCI. LL B programme’s well known objective is professional understanding of law and eligibility for enrolment into the bar. Statements of mission and objective of the college clearly manifest that we strive to prepare practitioners of law. LL.B Programme

This college is a mono faculty institution offering a three year law course. On Passing the third year University confers LL.B. Degree. The student is entitled to practice after enrolling into Bar Council as per the Council rules. The existing rules require All India Bar Examination to be cleared in order to receive the certificate of practice.
